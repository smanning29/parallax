ADVENTURES OUT WEST

Driving through the desert of Utah, you are surrounded by nothing but blue sky and orange dirt for miles. Then, along the horizon, great red rock buttes begin to come in to your vision. You stare in awe at the majestic landforms carved by nature of thousands of years. The plants arround you start to look green and alive, and you start to get excited. After hours of driving all day, you're almost at your destination!
You lean out the window of the car to take a quick snapshot of the gorgeous nature around you.
The picture you took looks great! The road winds around some more as the red rock formations grow bigger and closer to your car.
You and your crew set up camp in a red rock canyon right next to the Colorado River.
After setting up your camp, you go to explore around and find local flora and fauna. There are cacti, some with bright pink and yellow blooms on top. You take a look, but are careful not to touch!
